About Us

        The Department of Computer Science & Technology is a home to those who take an interest to immerse into the World of Computers. Here, a Four-year journey awaits that is filled with exciting activities, fascinating events, adventurous days of Placement and many more. The Department that stands tall since 1993 , brings life into the college as each year the strength of students increase. Let's turn that strength into a Power for the Future.

  • Highly qualified and dedicated teachers
  • Specialised in major topics of CS and IT
  • State-of-the-art lab facilities to train the students for the present and future

       The beginning year itself saw the growth of the department, as a 4 year B.Tech programme in Computer Science and Engineering was offered with an intake of 60. The number of intake had increased to 90 from 2008 admissions . Our department have also seen the rise of many university rank holders. In addition to this, our students have successfully contested in many State and National Level technical events. Moreover the placement drive on-campus and off-campus interviews in various Central /State/PSUs and Multinational Companies. Faculty and staff of Computer Science Department are actively involved in consultancy work.

Events

           A Four year journey without some spice? Sounds boring! How about adding the right amount of fun along with learning?

That's what we focus in our Department Events. Be it Teranis , Quiz competitions, Hackathon or others, you'd always find something to learn while you're having fun!    Teranis is a Festival of the CS Department. A 2 - 3 day fest with music, dance and fun with informative stalls at every nook and corner. It is an opportunity that opens doors to workshops, seminars that are taken by highly qualified personnel, quizzes, hackathons and stalls on related topics that are arranged by the students themselves. It is also an opportunity for students to get closer among themselves as well as to the Department!
